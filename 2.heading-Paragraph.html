Coronavirus/Covid-19

Coronavirus is recently discovered a kind of virus.It causes diseases in mammals and birds.The name “coronavirus” is derived from Latin.The meaning of corona is “crown” or “wreath”.This virus causes cough,flu and fever.It rapidly destroys the respiratory system that causes death to the affected person.This fatal diseases caused by the coronavirus is named covid-19.It is totally an infectious disease.The disease was identified in December 2019 in Wuhan,China and has since spread globally.The common symptons of covid-19 are fever,cough,fatigue,breathing problem,shortness of breath and loss of smell and taste.Majority of the cases show mild symptons.The virus is primarily spread among people during close contact with affected people.The affected people produce small droplets by coughing,sneezing as well as talking.These droplets carrying coronavirus spread covid-19 among other people.Covid-19 vaccines is invented now,including Phizer-BioNTech,Moderna,AstraZeneca,Sputnik-V,Sinopharm,Sinovac.But we need to be aware.Preventive measures to reduce the chances of infection include staying at home,avoiding crowded places,keeping distance from others,washing hands with soap and water often and for at least 20 seconds and avoid touching the eyes,nose or mouth with unwashed hands.In fact,controlled lifestyle and awareness of mass people are very much important to get rid of coronavirus.
